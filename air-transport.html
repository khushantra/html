Air Transportation

Explore the environmental impact of air travel, including the effects of airplane emissions and innovations in green aviation.

Commercial Airplanes

Commercial airplanes are responsible for a significant portion of global CO₂ emissions due to fuel consumption and long flight durations.

  • CO₂ Emission: ~100-150 grams per passenger-kilometer
  • Fuel Type: Jet fuel (aviation gasoline)
  • Impact: High emissions, but newer models and sustainable fuels aim to reduce this impact

Private Planes

Private planes emit significantly more CO₂ per passenger due to fewer people per flight and inefficient fuel use.

  • CO₂ Emission: ~200-300 grams per passenger-kilometer
  • Fuel Type: Jet fuel, Aviation gasoline
  • Impact: Higher emissions compared to commercial flights
